1. Payment processing

Payments are processed by third-party providers (for example, card networks and digital wallets). Millo does not store full card numbers on its own servers. Availability of payment methods depends on your region, our compliance settings, and processor support.

2. Refunds

Refunds may be considered where required by law, where a duplicate charge occurred, where a paid digital good or service was not delivered due to a verified platform error, or where fraud is confirmed after review. Refunds are not guaranteed for change-of-mind purchases of digital content or virtual currency where terms at checkout stated otherwise.

Refund timing, if approved, typically follows the processor’s settlement cycle (often several business days). Millo may issue refunds as credits to your Millo wallet or as reversals to the original payment method, depending on the payment rail and policy in effect.

3. Disputes on Millo vs. payment network disputes

In-app or support “disputes” are operational cases for Millo staff to review (for example, order issues or policy questions). They do not replace your rights to open a dispute or chargeback with your bank or card network, where applicable. Those processes are governed by your agreement with your financial institution and network rules.

4. Chargebacks and abuse

Fraudulent chargebacks or misuse of payment dispute mechanisms may result in account suspension or termination, withholding of payouts pending investigation, and cooperation with processors and law enforcement where required.

5. Creator earnings and payouts

Creator payouts are subject to identity verification (KYC), tax information collection where required, fraud and compliance review, minimum payout thresholds, and processor payout availability. Payouts may be delayed or withheld when verification is incomplete, when suspicious activity is detected, or when required by law.

6. Fees

Millo may charge platform, processing, or subscription fees as disclosed at checkout or in creator agreements. Fees may change with notice as described in the Terms of Service.

7. Contact

For payment questions, use in-app support or the contact methods listed on https://milloapp.com.
