## 1. Data we collect

- Account information (e.g. email, username)
- Usage data (e.g. views, clicks)
- Payment metadata (not full card numbers)
- Device and IP address information
- **Device-related signals:** We collect device-related information such as **browser type**, **IP address**, and **system configuration** (for example screen resolution, time zone, or similar technical data) to **help prevent fraud** and **secure accounts**, in addition to operating the service.
- **Interaction patterns:** In line with our fraud and abuse protections, we may analyze **interaction patterns** (for example **mouse movement**, **clicks**, and **typing timing** — not the content of what you type) to detect automated or risky activity and **protect accounts**.
- **Login risk evaluation:** We evaluate sign-in attempts using **device**, **network** (such as IP-derived location where available), and **behavioral** signals, together with account history, to **protect accounts** and help **prevent unauthorized access** (including risk-based challenges such as verification codes or CAPTCHA when appropriate).

## 2. How we use data

- To provide and operate the service
- To improve recommendations and product experience
- For fraud prevention and security
- To meet legal and compliance obligations

## 3. Sharing

We may share data with:

- Payment processors
- Cloud and infrastructure providers
- Analytics tools (as described in our Cookie Policy and settings)

## 4. User rights

**Optional fingerprinting:** Where we offer it in product settings, you may **opt out of optional device fingerprint collection** for your account. If you opt out, we will not store optional client-provided fingerprint payloads tied to that preference. We may still process data that is necessary for **security**, **fraud prevention**, and **legal compliance** (such as IP address associated with a login or payment request).

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Delete** your data (subject to legal exceptions)
- **Request a copy / export** (data subject access request — DSAR)

Use in-product privacy settings and support where available. Contact: **privacy@milloapp.com**

## 5. Data retention

We retain data as long as necessary for **legal**, **regulatory**, and **legitimate operational** purposes, then delete or anonymize it in line with our retention schedule (to be detailed with counsel).

## 6. Security

We use technical and organizational measures such as **encryption**, **access controls**, and **monitoring** appropriate to the risk. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 7. International transfers

Data may be processed in **multiple countries**. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g. standard contractual clauses) as determined with counsel.
