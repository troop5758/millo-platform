## Payment processing

Payments are processed through third-party providers. Millo does not store full card numbers on its own servers. Available methods depend on region, compliance zone, and processor configuration.

## Refunds

Refunds may be issued when required by law, for duplicate charges, for verified non-delivery of a paid digital good or service due to platform error, or when fraud is confirmed after review. Refunds are generally not guaranteed for change-of-mind purchases of digital goods or wallet top-ups where checkout terms state final sale.

Approved refunds are executed via the original payment rail or as wallet credit where reversal is not possible; timing follows processor settlement (often multiple business days).

## Disputes vs. chargebacks

Cases opened in the Millo **disputes** UI are **operational** reviews. They do not replace card-network or bank **chargeback** or **wallet dispute** rights where those apply.

## Chargebacks

Users who abuse chargebacks or payment disputes may face account action, payout holds, and cooperation with processors.

## Creator earnings

Creators receive payouts only after applicable **KYC**, **tax** information, fraud checks, minimum thresholds, and processor payout readiness. The API enforces payout blocks when requirements are incomplete.

## Fees

Millo may charge platform or processing fees as disclosed at checkout or in creator terms.
