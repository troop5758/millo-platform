## Purpose

To prevent fraud, money laundering, and comply with regulations.

## When required

- payouts
- high-value transactions
- suspicious activity

## Verification includes

- government ID
- selfie verification
- address verification

## Providers

KYC is handled via third-party providers (e.g., Stripe Identity).

## Failure to verify

Users may be:

- restricted
- unable to withdraw funds
- suspended
