## Designated agent

Copyright-related notices must be sent to the **designated DMCA agent** listed on https://milloapp.com/legal/copyright.html (name, physical address, email). Non-DMCA mail may not receive a response.

## Takedown notice (17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(3))

A valid takedown notice should include, at minimum:

1. A physical or electronic signature of the person authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
3. Identification of the material claimed to be infringing and information reasonably sufficient to permit Millo to locate it (e.g. URL, stream or content ID).
4. Contact information for the complaining party (address, telephone, email).
5. A statement that the complaining party has a good faith belief that use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
6. A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that the complaining party is authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.

Notices may be submitted via the **DMCA form** at https://milloapp.com/legal/dmca or by email to the designated agent.

## Counter-notification (outline)

If content was removed or disabled due to a takedown notice, the uploader may submit a counter-notification that substantially complies with 17 U.S.C. § 512(g)(3), including identification of the material and its location prior to removal, contact information, consent to jurisdiction, and statements under penalty of perjury as required by law. Millo may restore material in accordance with applicable law unless the original notifier seeks a court order.

## Repeat infringer policy

Millo may terminate or restrict accounts of repeat infringers where appropriate under its policies and applicable law.
